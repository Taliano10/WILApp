## 📌 Overview

WELAP (Work-Integrated Learning Automation Platform) is a full-stack web application developed to solve communication and progress tracking challenges in the Work-Integrated Learning process at the University of Mpumalanga.

The platform connects **students**, **lecturers**, and **employers** on a single system, replacing the traditional manual placement process with an automated, transparent, and efficient workflow.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement

Before WELAP:
- Students were randomly assigned to firms without knowing details about salaries, accommodation, or work conditions
- Lecturers had no real-time visibility into student progress
- Employers lacked a standardized way to evaluate students
- Communication between stakeholders was fragmented and inefficient

---

## ✅ Features

### 👨‍🎓 For Students
- Browse available firms with detailed information
- Filter by location, salary, and accommodation availability
- View workplace reviews and ratings
- Apply to firms directly through the platform
- Track application status
- Upload progress reports (images, videos, documents)
- Receive real-time notifications

### 👨‍🏫 For Lecturers
- Real-time dashboard to monitor student progress
- View weekly curriculum-based reports
- Review student-submitted evidence (images, videos)
- Direct communication with students and employers
- Generate progress reports

### 🏢 For Employers
- Create and manage company profiles
- Post WIL vacancies
- Submit student evaluations (curriculum-based)
- View applicant profiles
- Provide workplace reviews
- Direct communication with university staff
